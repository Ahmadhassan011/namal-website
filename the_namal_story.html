Our Journey

Namal University is a story of vision, determination, and commitment to transforming lives through education.

Inception

Namal began as Namal Institute with a vision to provide accessible higher education to the people of Mianwali and surrounding areas. The founder's dream was to create opportunities for bright students who lacked access to quality education.

Growth & Expansion

Over the years, Namal expanded its academic programs to include Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Business Studies, and Mathematics. The university received recognition from the Higher Education Commission (HEC) of Pakistan.

Namal Knowledge City

The establishment of Namal Knowledge City marked a new chapter - a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art facilities, hostels, sports grounds, and renewable energy projects. The campus is designed to be sustainable and environmentally friendly.

Today

Today, Namal University stands as a symbol of excellence in education. Our graduates are making their mark in various industries, and our Time Bank program has become a model for community service and social responsibility in education.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our founding principles - providing quality education, fostering innovation, and creating leaders who will contribute to the development of Pakistan and beyond.
