About the Department

The Department of Electrical Engineering at Namal University offers a comprehensive BS Electrical Engineering program with state-of-the-art labs and experienced faculty. Our curriculum is designed to produce engineers who can contribute to the technological advancement of Pakistan.

Students have access to well-equipped labs including Analog & Digital Electronics Lab, Circuit & Communication Lab, Power Electronics Lab, and Physics & Control Lab.

  • MS Electrical Engineering
  • PhD Electrical Engineering
