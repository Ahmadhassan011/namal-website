About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at Namal University offers a comprehensive BS Computer Science program designed to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also equipped with the skills needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving technology industry.

Our curriculum is aligned with industry demands and focuses on practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge. Students have access to state-of-the-art computer labs and are encouraged to participate in research projects and hackathons.
